# Product Scope

## What AoTune Is

AoTune is:

- **Personal-first:** private usefulness comes before publishing or engagement.
- **Workspace-based:** each domain has focused tools, context, and artifact types.
- **Artifact-centered:** useful results persist beyond a single conversation.
- **Agent-assisted:** AI supports user-directed work rather than acting as an
  autonomous replacement for the user.
- **Bilingual:** the application is intended to support English and Chinese.
- **Extensible:** shared foundations can support multiple creative and research
  workspaces without forcing them into one generic workflow.

The core product loop is: enter a workspace, work toward a specific goal with
appropriate assistance, and keep or refine a structured artifact.

## What AoTune Is Not

AoTune is not:

- A social network, forum, public feed, or engagement platform.
- Only a lyrics learning application.
- A generic chatbot with interchangeable prompts.
- A lyrics scraping or content aggregation website.
- A pure image generation application.
- A fully autonomous agent system that acts without user direction.

Sharing and collaboration may be considered later, but they must remain
intentional extensions of private workspaces rather than redefine the product
around public attention.

## Current Scope

Phase 0 covers repository initialization and documentation. Phase 1 will
establish the Japanese Lyrics Learning workspace foundation. The remaining
workspaces are planned placeholders; database, agent, LLM, authentication, and
IAM implementations remain out of scope until explicitly scheduled.
