# Planned Workspaces

The five workspaces are equal parts of AoTune's long-term product vision. Their
implementation order does not define their strategic importance.

## Japanese Lyrics Learning

**Purpose:** Support deliberate Japanese study through user-provided music and
lyrics context.

**User goals:** Understand vocabulary, grammar, imagery, and cultural context;
retain useful notes; connect language learning with meaningful music.

**Possible artifacts:** Annotated excerpts, vocabulary notes, grammar cards,
translation observations, study summaries, and review lists.

**Current status:** Phase 1 implementation target.

**Future scope:** Agent-assisted analysis, study review flows, artifact linking,
and personal learning preferences. It must not become a lyrics scraping service.

## Cosplay Planning

**Purpose:** Turn character inspiration into an organized, achievable plan.

**User goals:** Research a character, collect references, plan construction or
sourcing, estimate effort, and track preparation.

**Possible artifacts:** Character research cards, costume breakdowns, material
lists, budgets, schedules, makeup notes, pose references, and convention plans.

**Current status:** Planned placeholder.

**Future scope:** Structured planning workflows, reference organization,
checklists, and links between visual concepts and practical preparation.

## Creative Studio

**Purpose:** Develop ACGN-inspired ideas into coherent creative directions.

**User goals:** Explore concepts, establish mood and narrative, iterate on
prompts, and preserve creative decisions.

**Possible artifacts:** Creative briefs, prompt sets, mood descriptions,
character concepts, story fragments, visual direction notes, and project boards.

**Current status:** Planned placeholder.

**Future scope:** Agent-assisted ideation and refinement across text and visual
references. Image generation may support a workflow but should not define it.

## Japanese Music Research

**Purpose:** Build structured personal knowledge about Japanese music.

**User goals:** Research artists, songs, albums, themes, influences, and cultural
context; connect findings across a personal collection.

**Possible artifacts:** Artist profiles, song research cards, album notes,
timelines, theme maps, source lists, and listening journals.

**Current status:** Planned placeholder.

**Future scope:** Source-aware research assistance, related-artifact discovery,
and personal music knowledge organization.

## Personal Branding Studio

**Purpose:** Translate personal interests and identity into consistent creative
presentation.

**User goals:** Clarify identity, define tone and visual direction, and prepare
materials for selected public or professional contexts.

**Possible artifacts:** Identity briefs, bios, profile copy, portfolio plans,
visual language guides, content themes, and presentation checklists.

**Current status:** Planned placeholder.

**Future scope:** Identity-aware recommendations, reusable brand systems, and
controlled exports while keeping private workspace content private by default.
